Who I Am?
I was born in Rwanda into a Christian family, where my upbringing was deeply influenced by the religious beliefs instilled by my parents and grandparents. My grandfather was a missionary, and my parents were devout churchgoers, which laid the foundation for my spiritual journey. From a young age, I was immersed in the teachings of Christianity, and as I grew older, I felt a calling to spread the message of faith.
Why I choose This
My Mission and Calling
My mission is to spread the message of salvation worldwide, guided by a deep commitment to the Great Commission. I aim to share the transformative love of Christ and invite all to experience the abundant life He offers.
Characteristics and Approach
As an evangelist, I embody compassion, authenticity, and unwavering faith. Rooted in reverence for sacred scripture and guided by the Holy Spirit, I engage with reverence and respect. Through listening, prayer, and storytelling, I aim to foster spiritual growth.
Activities and Outreach
My evangelistic endeavors involve a variety of activities such as preaching sermons, praying for the sick, sharing messages on social media, conducting Bible studies, and engaging in community outreach efforts. I aim to minister to the marginalized, comfort the brokenhearted, and empower believers to live out their faith boldly by extending God’s love and mercy to all.
Impact and Testimony
The true measure of my ministry is not in recognition but in the lives transformed by God’s grace. Through His mercy, I have witnessed countless souls liberated, restored, and empowered to fulfill their divine purpose. My testimony reflects the enduring power of God’s love to heal and redeem eternally.
